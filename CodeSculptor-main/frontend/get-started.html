🚀 How to Use CodeSculptor

1

Describe Your Algorithm

Type your algorithm in plain English. For example: "Sort the array [5, 3, 8, 1] using bubble sort" or "Find the Fibonacci number of 5". You don't need to know complex syntax!

2

Generate Pseudocode

Click the "Generate Pseudocode" button. Our AI engine will translate your words into structured, easy-to-read pseudocode instantly.

3

Watch & Learn

See your code come to life! The visualizer will animate the algorithm step-by-step, helping you understand the logic visually. You can play, pause, and even listen to voice narration.
