How to Use CodeSculptor

Your step-by-step guide to algorithmic mastery

1

Describe Your Algorithm

Type your algorithm in plain English. For example: Sort the array [5, 3, 8, 1] using bubble sort or Find the Fibonacci number of 5. You don't need to know complex syntax – just describe what you want to do!

2

Generate Visualization

Click the "Generate Visualization" button. Our AI engine will translate your words into structured, easy-to-read pseudocode and create a step-by-step visualization instantly.

3

Watch & Learn

See your code come to life! The visualizer will animate the algorithm step-by-step on the left, while the pseudocode is highlighted on the right. You can play, pause, reset, and even listen to voice narration to understand the logic visually and audibly.
